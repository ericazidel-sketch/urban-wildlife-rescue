Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of wildlife enthusiasts committed to rescuing, rehabilitating, and rehoming raccoons in need. From curious kits to seasoned snack bandits, every raccoon in our care receives expert attention, enrichment, and lots of snacks.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a safe and nurturing environment for raccoons while educating the public about responsible wildlife interaction. We believe every raccoon deserves a second chance—and maybe a secure trash can.

What We Do

  • Rescue injured or abandoned raccoons
  • Provide medical care and rehabilitation
  • Match raccoons with loving (and patient) adopters
  • Educate communities about wildlife coexistence

Why Raccoons?

Raccoons are intelligent, curious, and full of personality. While they may have a reputation for mischief, they are incredibly adaptable animals that thrive with proper care and attention.
